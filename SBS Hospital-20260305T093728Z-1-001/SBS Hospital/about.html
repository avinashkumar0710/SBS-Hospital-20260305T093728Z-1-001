About SBS Hospital

Committed to excellence in healthcare — serving Bhilai with compassion, expertise and integrity.

Our Story

Born from a Commitment to
Better Healthcare

SBS Hospital — officially Shri Bira Singh Multispeciality Hospital Pvt. Ltd. — was incorporated on January 5, 2021 with one clear purpose: to bring affordable, world-class multispeciality healthcare to the people of Bhilai and the broader Durg district of Chhattisgarh.

Located on G.E. Road, Camp 2, the hospital occupies a strategic position that makes it accessible to patients from across the region. From the very first day, SBS Hospital has maintained the highest standards of clinical care, hygiene, and patient comfort.
