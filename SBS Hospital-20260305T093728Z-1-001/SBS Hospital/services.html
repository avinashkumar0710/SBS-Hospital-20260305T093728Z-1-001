Our Medical Services

Expert care across 6+ specialties, backed by state-of-the-art infrastructure and a dedicated medical team.

Specialties

Comprehensive Healthcare Under One Roof

From routine check-ups to complex surgeries — SBS Hospital is equipped to serve every medical need.

🦴

Orthopaedics

Our orthopaedic team provides comprehensive musculoskeletal care with the latest surgical techniques.

  • Fracture management & bone care
  • Total knee & hip replacement
  • Sports injury rehabilitation
  • Spinal disc & spine disorders
  • Arthroscopic surgery
👶

Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Complete women's health services from adolescence through menopause — with expert maternal care.

  • Antenatal & prenatal care
  • Normal & C-section deliveries
  • Family planning counselling
  • Gynaecological disorders treatment
  • Laparoscopic surgery
❤️

Cardiology

Advanced heart care services with modern diagnostic and monitoring equipment to ensure cardiac health.

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Hypertension management
  • Heart failure treatment
  • Cardiac risk assessment
🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Expert diagnosis and treatment of complex neurological and brain conditions.

  • Stroke diagnosis & management
  • Epilepsy treatment
  • Spinal cord injury care
  • Headache & migraine clinic
  • Peripheral neuropathy
🏃

Physiotherapy

Evidence-based rehabilitation to restore movement, manage pain, and improve quality of life.

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Musculoskeletal physiotherapy
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Sports physiotherapy
  • Pain management therapy
🏥

ICU & Critical Care

Round-the-clock intensive care unit with advanced life support and monitoring for critical patients.

  • 24×7 ICU monitoring
  • Mechanical ventilation support
  • Multi-organ failure management
  • Post-operative critical care
  • Sepsis management
🚑

Emergency & Trauma Care

24×7 emergency response with ambulance services and a dedicated trauma team on standby always.

  • 24×7 emergency department
  • Trauma & accident care
  • Ambulance services
  • Life-support stabilisation
  • Emergency surgery readiness
💊

In-House Pharmacy

Fully stocked pharmacy on premises for immediate access to prescribed medicines and health products.

  • All prescribed medications available
  • 24-hour pharmacy support
  • Generic medicine options
  • Surgical supplies & consumables
  • Expert pharmacist consultation
